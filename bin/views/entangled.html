biton entangled demo

bitonproject.org

  • Start a biton client within this browser tab
  • Generate a public keypair and biton identity
  • Join the global test network, discovering other users through BitTorrent Mainline DHT
  • For each connection add a circle in the graph and perform a Noise_XX handshake
  • Click on a circle to swap the entangled color! (This will also swap the color of your circle at the browser of the other user)
  • Hint* See your browser console for debug messages

What information will others learn about me?

Running this demo will expose your IP address to other biton entangled users and to the BitTorrent DHT network.
