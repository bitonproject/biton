biton entangled demo

  • Start a biton client within this browser tab
  • Generate a public keypair and respective biton identity
  • Join the global test network, discovering other users using BitTorrent Mainline DHT
  • Add new peers as circles in the graph and perform a Noise_XX handshake
  • Click on their circle to change your entangled state (will update the color of your circle on their browser!)
  • Hint* See your browser console for debug messages

What information will others learn about me?

Running this demo will expose your IP address to other biton entangled users and to the BitTorrent DHT network.
